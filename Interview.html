UPSC Guide

UPSC Interview (Personality Test)

Your final step to becoming a Civil Servant

1. What is the UPSC Interview?

The UPSC Interview is the final stage of the Civil Services Examination. It focuses on assessing your personality, maturity, and decision-making ability rather than academic knowledge.

2. Purpose of the Interview

This test is designed to understand your character, leadership, ethics, presence of mind, and suitability for public service roles.

3. Marks and Weightage

The Personality Test carries 275 marks. With Mains (1750 marks), the total is 2025. The interview can make a major difference in final rankings.

Expect questions from your DAF, educational background, hobbies, ethical case studies, governance, current issues, and your thoughts on national policies.

5. Tips from Toppers

Be authentic, composed, and well-informed. Practice mock interviews, read newspapers, and understand every word in your DAF. Your attitude matters more than accuracy.

6. Dress Code

Men: Light shirt, formal trousers, shoes. Tie/blazer optional.
Women: Saree, salwar suit, or formal western wear. Keep it neat, modest, and confident.
