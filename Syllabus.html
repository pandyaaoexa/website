UPSC Civil Services Examination Stages

1. UPSC Prelims Exam

Purpose: To screen candidates for the Mains exam.

Format:

  • General Studies Paper I (200 marks)
  • CSAT Paper II (200 marks, qualifying)

Duration: Each paper is 2 hours.

Marking: Negative marking for wrong answers in Paper I.

Key Focus: Current affairs, general knowledge, and aptitude.
